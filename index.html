The Process

Ten phases. One repeatable rhythm.

From the first stakeout to your certificate of occupancy, every Terra project follows the same disciplined sequence — refined over years of single-family work in New Jersey.

  1. 01

    Site & Stakeout

    Survey, soils, setbacks, and the first walk of the land.

  2. 02

    Design & Permitting

    Architectural drawings, engineering, town approvals, and zoning.

  3. 03

    Foundation

    Excavation, footings, foundation walls, and waterproofing.

  4. 04

    Framing

    Floors, walls, roof — the moment the house takes its shape.

  5. 05

    Mechanicals

    Plumbing, electrical, HVAC, low-voltage rough-in.

  6. 06

    Envelope & Roofing

    Insulation, exterior cladding, windows, and a tight roof.

  7. 07

    Interior Finishes

    Drywall, trim, flooring, cabinetry, tile, and paint.

  8. 08

    Kitchen & Baths

    Stone, fixtures, appliances — the rooms clients live in most.

  9. 09

    Site & Landscape

    Driveway, grading, hardscape, and planting.

  10. 10

    Punch & C of O

    Final walks, inspections, and the keys in your hand.
